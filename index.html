The Reason We Choose Mossanite

Moissanite is engineered for the purpose of being a better substitute for natural diamonds by scientists. It is the second hardest gem to diamonds that exists, and even more brilliant than diamonds.
While remaining 100% ethical & sustainable, moissanite is an affordable alternative with very similar characteristics as diamonds.

History of Moissanite

Moissanite was discoverd by a French chemist and pharmacist, HHenri Moissan in 1893. It was discoverd in a meteor crater in Arizona. Natural moissanite is extremely rare to find, therefore all moissanite sold are lab-made.

Moissanite is made of silicon carbide mineral, and its crystalline structure is held together with a strong bond, similar to diamonds.
